Playing the guitar is one of my passions.

Playing the guitar is one of my favorite hobbies! I never tire of strumming those strings and playing my favorite riffs. Whenever I have spare time to myself, I pick up my guitar and start learning new songs. Not only do I get an incredible sense of satisfaction from hitting each note right, but it also helps me fill my free time productively. As a bonus, I get to share what I've learned with friends and family when we're together. Nothing compares to the feeling of everyone singing along with a song you wrote yourself or played perfectly on the guitar!
